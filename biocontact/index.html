Bio:

Hi, I am Yohan and I am a French & English-speaking actor and voice-over artist currently based in Paris, France (during Covid times).

I like to make my life simple. I like to deal with things one step at a time. I have a pretty clear picture of what I want to achieve in life. I like to be on stage in front of a big audience, which could be performing in a big theatre, or presenting a TED talk about the power of your mind!

My dream apartment is a minimally furnished penthouse of a small building, with floor to ceiling windows and a grand piano facing the beautiful view of [insert city name here]. And you’re invited, of course.

I value work + having free time to enjoy simple things, like cooking, taking naps, looking at trees, talking over a cup of tea, or over quality food and a glass of wine. I like to own a small number of things.

I act. I picture myself doing it most of the time + doing business creatively the other part of the time. I am straightforward and diplomatic in my way of communicating. I adore Hugo Boss. I rarely buy Hugo Boss.

I enjoy conveying one meaningful message at a time. I seek balance, fairness and peace. I am a good adviser. I am good at impersonating locals, organizing, following my intuition and playing the piano.

I like healthy food and clean counters. I strive to experience the greater and uplifting spheres of society while staying in sync with my values and principles. I like Charlize Theron, Joe Hisaishi, Hayao Miyasaki, Tilda Swinton, Ralph Fiennes and Stanley Tucci [to name a few]. I cried watching The Last 5 Years, Billy Elliot, Rent, The Imitation Game, Black Swan, Big Fish and Spirited Away [to name a few]. I like nature. I love my family. I love love.

I love achieving things. I work effectively standing up and with deadlines. I take pleasure in taking days off from technology. I like the Japanese culture. I won an award for performing the role of a sadistic psychopath, and now you’re slightly backing off from the screen! Rest assured, I love Disney.

I value communication. I love re-reading what I wrote a year ago. I will make a great James Bond villain. I like change. I love life.

For a more formal version of myself: I am a graduate from the Lee Strasberg Theatre & Film Institute in New York, I have a Master’s Degree in Quantification of Financial Risks from Paris Dauphine University, I am a graduate from “Le Wagon”, a computer programming intensive based in Paris, I won the award for “Best Monologue” at the Winter Film Awards in 2012, I have been a contestant on “Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?”, “The Weakest Link” & “1 vs 100”. One of my absolute dreams would be to play “Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?”.
